Research Design

Theoretical Frameworks

TAM Model

Davis (1989). Explains adoption via Perceived Usefulness (Credit for small buys) and Ease of Use (Scan vs Swipe).

UTAUT

Venkatesh (2003). Adds Social Influence (QR visibility) and Facilitating Conditions (Smartphone penetration).
